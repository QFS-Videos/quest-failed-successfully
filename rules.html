Hidden Icon Challenge Rules

Last updated: July 2026

1. No Purchase Necessary

No purchase is necessary to enter or win.

2. How to Enter

  1. Watch the featured Quest Failed Successfully YouTube video.
  2. Find the hidden Quest Failed Successfully icon.
  3. Email your entry to contest@failed-successfully.com.

Include your YouTube username, the video title or link, and the timestamp where the icon appears.

3. Entry Limit

Limit one entry per person, per video.

4. Entry Period

Entries must be received within 7 days after the video is published, unless a different deadline is stated in that video's description.

5. Prizes

Current prizes are a $20 Nintendo eShop gift card for first place and a $10 Nintendo eShop gift card for second place. Prizes may change for future challenges.

6. Winner Selection

Winners will be selected from eligible entries that correctly identify the hidden icon. Unless otherwise stated, winners are selected based on the order correct entries are received by email.

7. Comments

Please do not post the timestamp or location of the hidden icon in the YouTube comments. Comments may be moderated to keep the challenge spoiler-free.

8. Eligibility

Open to participants who are legally permitted to receive digital gift cards in their country. Void where prohibited. Participants under 18 should obtain permission from a parent or guardian.

9. Privacy

Email addresses and submitted information will only be used to administer the challenge and contact winners.

10. YouTube and Nintendo Disclaimer

This promotion is not sponsored, endorsed, administered by, or associated with YouTube or Nintendo.

11. Right to Modify

Quest Failed Successfully reserves the right to modify, suspend, or cancel the challenge if necessary due to technical issues, fraud, or other circumstances.

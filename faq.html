FAQ

Where do I submit my entry?

Email your timestamp, YouTube username, and the video title or link to contest@failed-successfully.com.

Should I post the timestamp in comments?

No. Please keep the location spoiler-free so everyone can enjoy the challenge.

Can I still comment?

Yes. Comments are encouraged. Just avoid revealing the timestamp or location.

How are winners picked?

Eligible correct entries are reviewed by email. Unless otherwise stated, winners are selected based on the order correct entries are received.
